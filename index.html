I am on a quest to learn how to build physical things.

I have come to believe that working in the digital realm is fun, but it is nowhere near as hard and impactful as what can be done in the physical world.

Right now I’m making Waffle Bot.

I make waffles for my partner every morning. Even though all I do is take them out of the freezer, toast them, and cover them in awesome toppings, I am lazy. Waffle Bot will carry them from the toaster to the plate for me.
